Strawberry Mango Bubble Tea

About the dish:

This bubble tea has the taste and texture of a smoothie. It is thick, fruity and naturally sweet. A fun, non-alcoholic drink for adults and kids. It is healthy as strawberries and mangoes are loaded with lots of fibre, vitamins and minerals while milk is packed with calcium and vitamin D.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup strawberries (chopped)
  • 1 cup chopped mango
  • 1/2 cup Boba (large tapioca pearls)

Directions:

  1. Place the strawberries and mangoes in the blender.
  2. Puree it till smooth. Strain the mixture through the strainer and discard the pulp.
  3. Put the juice in the fridge to chill.
  4. Boil the Boba pearls in water for about 5 minutes and let it cool completely.
  5. Stir the chilled juice and milk together.
  6. Pour the same into glasses and divide the cooked boba pearls as per the number of servings.
  7. Enjoy the same with a straw.
