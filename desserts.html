Chocolate Hazelnut Ice-cream Cheese Cake

About the dish:

It combines the best of both the worlds-cakes as well as ice creams. This is a rich and creamy cheesecake which is ideal for a dinner party. Also, it will help you to satiate your sweet tooth cravings in an easy manner.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Honeynut Cornflakes
  • 2 jars of Hazelnut Chocolate Spread of 400g each
  • 2 full fat Cream Cheese of 180g each
  • 1 tablespoon roasted and chopped Hazelnuts

Directions:

  1. Put the cornflakes and half a jar of chocolate hazelnut spread in a bowl and beat to combine.Press the mix into the base of a 23cm springform tin.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth, then fold in the remaining chocolate hazelnut spread.
  3. Smooth onto the cornflake base, wrap tightly in cling film and freeze overnight.
  4. Remove from the freezer 30 mins before serving, or until you can cut it easily with a sharp knife.
  5. Serve in slices with hazelnuts sprinkled over.
